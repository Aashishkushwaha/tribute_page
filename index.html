Timeline of M.S Dhoni

  • 1981 - Born in Ranchi, Jharkhand (India)
  • 1995 - Became the regular wicketkeeper at the Commando cricket club.
  • 1997 - Picked for the 1997/98 season Vinoo Mankad Trophy Under-16 Championship.
  • 1998-99 - Dhoni was included in the Bihar U-19 squad.
  • 1999-2000 - Dhoni made his Ranji Trophy debut for Bihar.
  • 2001-2003 - Dhoni's talent was discovered via the BCCI's small-town talent-spotting initiative TRDW. Dhoni was discovered by TRDO Prakash Poddar, captain of Bengal in the 1960s, when he saw Dhoni play for Jharkhand at a match in Jamshedpur
  • 2003-2004 - Dhoni was part of the East Zone squad that won the Deodhar Trophy.
  • 2003-2004 - Dhoni was picked for the India A squad for a tour of Zimbabwe and Kenya.
  • 2004-2005(International Debut) - Dhoni was picked in the ODI squad for the Bangladesh tour in 2004/05.
  • 2005 (Test Debut) - Dhoni made his test debut by replacing Dinesh Karthik in December 2005 as the Indian teams 'Test wicket-keeper'.
  • 2006 - #1 in the ICC ODI Rankings for batsmen on 20 April 2006 becoming the fastest batsman to do so, in 42 innings.
  • 2006 - Dhoni scored his maiden century in the second Test at Faisalabad.
  • 2007 - Dhoni was awarded an 'A' grade contract in June 2007.
  • 2007 - Dhoni was named the captain of Indian squad for the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 held in South Africa in September 2007
  • 2007 - Under the captaincy of Dhoni India won the first ever edition of T-20 World cup in 2007.
  • 2007 - He went on to become the ODI captain of the Indian team for the seven-match ODI series against Australia in September 2007.
  • 2008 - He made his debut as full-time Test captain of India during the fourth and final Test against Australia at Nagpur in November 2008, replacing Anil Kumble who was injured in the third test and who then announced his retirement.
  • 2009 - Dhoni took his first wicket in international cricket on 30 September 2009. He bowled Travis Dowlin of the West Indies during a match of the 2009 Champions Trophy.
  • 2009 - Dhoni topped the ICC ODI Batsman rankings for several months in 2009.
  • 2009 - Dhoni scored two centuries in Sri Lanka's tour of India in 2009.
  • 2010 - Dhoni married Sakshi Singh Rawat, his schoolmate in DAV Jawahar Vidya Mandir, Shyamali. a native of Dehradun, Uttarakhand, on 4 July 2010.
  • 2011 - Under the captaincy of Dhoni India lift their second World Cup title since 1983. He scored 91 not out, finishing the game with a historical six and claiming the Man of the Match award.
  • 2013 - Under the captaincy of Dhoni India lift ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.
  • 2014-2015 - He played his last series in the 2014â€“15 season in India's tour of Australia captaining India in the second and third tests
  • 2008 - Dhoni was contracted by the Chennai Super Kings for US$1.5 million. This made him the most expensive player in the IPL for the first season auctions.
  • 2010 & 2012 & 2018 - Chennai Super Kings won the 2010 and 2011 and 2018Indian Premier League titles and the 2010 and 2014 Champions League T20 titles.
